Its all about the Money
"Expensive" and "stressful" might be two things that come to mind when you think of wedding planning, but they don't have to be.
find the budgeting mistakes you should avoid in order to make your wedding planning process as fun and cost-efficient as possible.

Find the perfect Location that fits your theme
Since a wedding day is an once-in-a-lifetime experience, you want to make everything special. From the music to the flowers to the guests, you are building memories that will last forever.
Included in these menus will be the venue. There are several types to choose from. You can go with something simple like a church or community hall.
You can select from a wide variety of catering halls or hotel ballrooms. You can also choose a distinct wedding venue that will make your day truly special.
It can be on a beautiful landscape or in a unique building. Whatever the setting, get the most out of your venue for you and your guests.
